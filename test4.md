# Question Paper — Chapter: Growing Up
**Total Marks: 30** | **Time: 1 Hour**

**Instructions:** All questions are compulsory. Read each question carefully before answering. Marks are indicated against each question.

**Section A — Choose the Correct Answer (5 × 1 = 5 marks)**

**1.** **a.** Who is the first woman amputee to climb Mount Everest? (Arunima Sinha / Mithali Raj / Kalpana Chawla) **b.** Who is the animal rights activist named UN Messenger for Peace? (Mary Kom / M Poorna / Jane Goodall) **c.** In which year did Tawakkol Karman receive the Nobel Peace Prize? (2001 / 2011 / 1921) **d.** Who among the following is a first woman fighter pilot in the Indian Air Force? (Seema Singh / Avani Chaturvedi / Priya Sharma) **e.** Which statement is not correct? (Everyone is free to make their own choices / Men and women can choose a career of their choice / Only women should do household chores)

**Section B — Fill in the Blanks (5 × 1 = 5 marks)**

**2.** **a.** We should respect everyone's __________. **b.** Children should take __________ before taking things. **c.** Girls and boys should play with each other and respect the __________ of the game. **d.** Tawakkol Karman is from __________. **e.** __________ was the first woman of Indian origin to fly in space.

**Section C — True or False (5 × 1 = 5 marks)**

**3.** **a.** Kalpana Chawla was the first woman amputee to climb Mount Everest. **b.** Mithali Raj is the highest run-scorer in women's international cricket. **c.** Girls do not join the armed forces. **d.** Girls and boys should be treated equally. **e.** Everyone has the right to equal opportunities for education.

**Section D — Match the Following (5 × 1 = 5 marks)**

**4.** **a.** Mary Kom **b.** PV Sindhu **c.** Tawakkol Karman **d.** Kalpana Chawla **e.** Arunima Sinha — *(Match with: women's rights, astronaut, badminton, Mount Everest, boxing)*

**Section E — Short Answer Questions (5 × 2 = 10 marks)**

**5.** **a.** How should boys and girls treat each other in school? **b.** How was work divided between men and women in the past? **c.** What kind of activities are done by women these days? **d.** Who is Mary Kom? **e.** Name the three air force fighter pilots of the Indian Air Force.
