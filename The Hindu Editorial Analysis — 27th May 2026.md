## ERROR DETECTION — Grammar Rules Applied

**Question 1 — Answer: (B)** — Rule: Neither...nor (Proximity Rule). "Employees" is plural and nearest to the verb, so "was" should be "were."

**Question 2 — Answer: (E) No error** — Rule: "The number of" takes a singular verb ("has decreased").

**Question 3 — Answer: (A)** — Rule: Compound subjects as a single unit. "Rice and curry" represents one meal idea, so it takes a singular verb ("is").

**Question 4 — Answer: (B)** — Rule: Relative Pronoun Agreement. "That" refers to "factors" (plural), so "determines" should be "determine."

**Question 5 — Answer: (B)** — Rule: Single person with multiple titles. One article "My" = one person with multiple roles, so "have come" should be "has come."
